# Open Questions trace

Mirror of `ARCHITECTURE.md` `## Open Questions`, with status tracking
beyond what fits in the main architecture doc.

Maintained manually as ADR proposals and acceptances land. The
`$adr-proposal` and `$adr-acceptance` skills include a step that
updates the matching row.

## Schema

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `Question` | Short title (matches `ARCHITECTURE.md`) |
| `Status` | `Open` / `Resolved by ADR-NNNN` / `Deferred to M<n>` |
| `LastReviewed` | ISO 8601 date |
| `Notes` | Free-text |
